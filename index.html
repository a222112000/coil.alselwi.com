# Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy We are committed to protecting children from sexual abuse and exploitation. Our app enforces strict safety guidelines, content monitoring, and user reporting mechanisms to prevent CSAE. ## Reporting Users can report abusive content or behavior via [support@example.com]. ## Monitoring We use automated moderation and manual review to detect and remove CSAE content. ## Enforcement Violation of our policy results in account bans and reported cases are escalated to authorities when required. _Last updated: April 2025_
